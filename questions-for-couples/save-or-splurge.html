Questions about save-or-splurge

Finances can be a tricky topic, but discussing spending habits together can help avoid conflict and set shared goals. Learning when to save and when to splurge enables couples to feel financially secure and united in their financial aspirations. By aligning your financial goals, you can create a stable foundation for shared dreams, whether it’s travel, a home, or future plans. Couples who approach finances together can enjoy a more harmonious relationship and feel empowered by their mutual success.
  • Would your partner prefer to save money by taking budget-friendly vacation or splurge on a luxury getaway?
  • Is your partner more inclined to save money by buying budget-friendly furniture and decor or splurge on high-end, quality items?
  • Do your partner prefer to save money by finding free or low-cost entertainment options or splurge on tickets to concerts, shows, or events?

Questions About Red Flags

Identifying and discussing red flags can help couples address potential issues before they become serious problems. By being proactive, partners can set boundaries and develop strategies to prevent unhealthy behaviors. This conversation fosters transparency and trust, ensuring both partners feel safe and respected. Addressing red flags early on helps build a strong foundation for a healthy and lasting relationship.
  • Is having a really short or very long history of relationships a red flag?
  • Is it a red flag if you never get introduced to your partner's friends and family?
  • Is returning texts and calls extremely slowly a red flag?
